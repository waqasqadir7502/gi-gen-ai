# Module 1: Foundations of Physical AI

Welcome to Module 1 of the Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics course. This module provides the foundational knowledge you need to understand Physical AI concepts, their historical development, and the mathematical foundations that underpin this exciting field.

## Overview

In this module, you will learn:
- What Physical AI is and how it differs from traditional AI
- The historical development and key milestones in Physical AI
- The mathematical foundations necessary for understanding Physical AI systems
- How to set up your simulation environment for hands-on learning

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:
1. Explain the fundamental concepts of Physical AI
2. Describe the evolution of Physical AI from early concepts to current state
3. Apply basic mathematical concepts relevant to Physical AI systems
4. Set up and configure a simulation environment for Physical AI experiments

## Module Structure

This module consists of 4 chapters:
1. **Chapter 1.1**: Introduction to Physical AI Concepts
2. **Chapter 1.2**: History and Evolution of Physical AI
3. **Chapter 1.3**: Basic Mathematics for Physical AI
4. **Chapter 1.4**: Simulation Environment Setup

Each chapter builds upon the previous one, providing a comprehensive foundation for the more advanced topics in subsequent modules.

## Getting Started

Begin with Chapter 1.1 to understand the core concepts of Physical AI. Each chapter includes hands-on exercises to reinforce your learning through practical application.
